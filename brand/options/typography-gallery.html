Katalogos — Body Font Pairings

8 body-font candidates rendered in the actual product UI: hero, navigation bar, body paragraph, form, data table. Heading is locked to Manrope 500. Palette is locked to P-01 Terracotta Warm. Tell me the ID you like (e.g. TY-A).

How to read this: Each card uses Manrope for the H2/H3 (lines like "Truffle Tagliatelle Menu" / "Build, publish, and share your menu in minutes."), then renders everything else in the candidate body font. Pay attention to: paragraph readability, data-table number rendering, form-label tone (warm/clinical), nav-link weight. The right body font is the one that fades into the background while still looking distinctive.
Locked context: Manrope (heading) · P-01 Terracotta Warm (palette) · "Your menu, online — in minutes." (tagline) · KW-02 wordmark.
