Katalogos — Palette Options

Six restaurant-flavored palettes, each with 8 colors (primary, accent, gray-900, gray-700, gray-300, gray-100, success, error). Each card shows the swatches, then a preview hero rendered in that palette so you can see the wordmark + tagline in context. Tell me the ID you like (e.g. P-03).

What "primary" and "accent" mean here: primary is the dominant brand color used on buttons, links, headers; accent is the secondary highlight used sparingly for focus / important states. Grays are the neutral scale for text and surfaces. Success/error are semantic states (always green/red but adjusted to fit the palette's warmth).
