Katalogos — Wordmark & Tagline Options

Same 12 fonts and 5 voice categories as Erevna's gallery (so you can compare across both products), inflected for the restaurant / menu vertical. Tell me the IDs you like (e.g. KW-04, KT-02).

How to read this: KW = Katalogos Wordmark, KT = Katalogos Tagline. Each card shows "Katalogos" rendered in 12 different Google Fonts at 3 weights. Cards with a GREEK ✓ badge correctly render Κατάλογος — others fall back to your system Greek font for that line. Wordmark sizing here is 56px (vs Erevna's 64px) because "Katalogos" is longer — visual weight stays comparable.
